How to use this hub

This program is structured into three learning tracks. Pick a role to see the job through one lens, pick a technology to go deep on a skill, then do the labs to prove it.

Quick Start

Don't know where to start? Try this:

  1. Pick your role above (or use the sidebar) — read it in 15 minutes
  2. Pick one technology that role uses most — read it in 30 minutes
  3. Do a lab from that technology guide — 30–60 minutes, hands-on
  4. Join a capstone in labs/ — 2–4 hours, end-to-end scenario
